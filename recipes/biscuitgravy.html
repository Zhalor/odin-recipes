Biscuits and Gravy

Hearty Biscuits with Sausage Gravy!

This hearty plate of biscuts with sausage gravy is the perfect meal to start a weekend morning!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of buttermilk biscuits
  • 6 cups whole milk
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1lb ground breakfast sausage
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook biscuits to package directions. While biscuits are cooking, prepare gravy.
  2. Coat pan lightly with oil and set heat to medium.
  3. Add ground sausage and chop into pieces with spatula.
  4. Once sausage is cooked, stir in flour to coat sausage. Let cook for 2-3 minutes to remove rawness.
  5. Add 2 cups of milk and stir. Let gravy cook until thickened.
  6. Repeat step 5 until milk is gone.
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Serve and enjoy!
